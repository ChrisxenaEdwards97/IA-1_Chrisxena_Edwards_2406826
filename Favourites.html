My Favourites

Here are some of my favourite songs, books, and anime that inspire and motivate me.

🎵 Favourite Music

  1. “Stronger (What Doesn’t Kill You)” – Kelly Clarkson
    I love this song because it reminds me that challenges make me tougher and more resilient, no matter what comes my way.
  2. "Eternity"- Alex Warren
    It’s my reflection song — it reminds of my Grandfather who is in a better place now. The regrets I have for not being able to support him before he past. A reminder that my parents are still here and some day they will be depending on me. A reminder of what i am working for.
  3. “Unstoppable” – Sia
    This song’s energy and message about confidence and strength keep me focused and empowered during stressful times.

📚 Favourite Books

Twelve Pillars(New Favourite) – Jim Rohn & Chris Widener

Suprisingly, I actually enjoyed reading this. It taught me about goal-setting, time management, and personal discipline, principles I now will try to implement daily.

Atomic Habits – James Clear

This book helps me stay consistent and form small habits that lead to big success in studies and life.

The Phsycology of Money – Morgan Housel

This book has changed my way of life financially. It should me my relationship with money and why i acted towards it the way i previously did. I believe it is a book that everyone should read at least once.

🎌 Favourite Anime

One piece

Beautiful animation and emotional storytelling about perseverance and family and friendship bonds.It has comedy,adventure and has many tear jerking moments that leaves the viewer wanting more.

Ouran Highschool Host Club

I've always been a romantic girly so, although the animation on this show isn't top notch, the story line will always have a place in my heart.

Spy × Family

It's fun and wholesome it combines action, love, and humor in a perfect balance.
